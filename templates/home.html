About Calamity Control


Calamity Control is an all-in-one portal that is ready to work at any given moment, at any given time.

We are a group of 3 CS students who built this application as a part of the NIT Trichy Pragyan 2016 Hackathon, and we built it for one simple reason. We wanted to save people, as much as our laptops allow us to do it.

The application and website, in unison, helps by allowing you to report a calamity in your area and also mention it's severity. This, bundled with the amazing power of crowd sourced contribution, we're able to showcase the safest and most dangerous areas at any given time.

For those of you even *without internet connections*, we provide a gateway for you to call our service and initiate a report almost immediately. This could prove to be useful to many stranded in various data-less situations. Our web platform is much more feature rich, and yet, streamlined to work efficiently and quickly in the time of need.

This is what you get when you use Calamity Control:

  • Instant Reporting of Calamities, online or offline
  • Detailed Report Filing
  • Timeline of Reports, including photos, videos, and tweets being shared by thousands
  • Heat Map of all the endangered areas, across the world
  • Directory of donators willing to donate items for the cause
  • Directory of volunteers willing to spend their time for the cause
  • Emergency Contact Services, at a moment's touch

We made this to help the world, so please support us by sharing when in case of a calamity. Let's put the technology we have in our palms to good use, and help people out there! :)

Thank you for using Calamity Control.
